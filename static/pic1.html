About Us

Hotel KLG Starlite, an epitome of Luxury, and Relaxation.

Striking a balance between traveller’s stay and business parties, Hotel KLG Starlite is the ultimate Tricity’s luxury lifestyle destination – an iconic luxury business hotel creating memorable moments each time; everytime! While traditions remain, such as the tea of Darjeeling, Art décor and the iconic European style of restrooms, the exciting new offerings at this luxurious business hotel include the elegant and refined COEN – All Day Cafe & Bar, The Beautiful fitness boutique, The Banquets and the Conference Halls.

KLG Starlite offers distinctive guestrooms, including four luxurious Starlite suites, which boast the luxurious beds and finest furnishing and all the other 4 star amenities with prompt service, more than any of the luxury business hotel in the Tricity. The Starlite Suites are designed to be reflective class and modernity, each of the suites features contemporary décor that is elegant and timeless complemented by city views. Luxury and elegance are complemented by discreet placement of the latest technology thoughtfully integrated throughout each room bringing guests high-definition television and wireless high-speed internet access.

At a single call, guests can order In-Room Dining, make restaurant reservations, communicate directly with the Concierge; requests wake-up calls, and even check airline schedules and print boarding pass. It’s Hotel KLG Starlite’s magnificent public spaces that set it apart as the quintessential grand palace. The opulent Starlite Banquets have been meticulously built to make the day special and unforgettable. Lavish social and business events and unforgettable weddings continue to be held in these luxurious spaces.
