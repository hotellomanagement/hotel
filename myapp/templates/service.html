KITCHEN

The Restaurant Table and Kitchen Management software module within hotello Hotel Management System can bring more convenience for customers and staff alike. This module can be customised to allow for online restaurant reservation system so that customers can reserve tables from anywhere with internet access. Recording of customers’ names and table number allocation can be done through the system without needing a separate restaurant reservation software. It can also act as kitchen and restaurant software. Food orders from diners can be converted to kitchen orders, to inform kitchen staff about dishes to prepare. More functions of this module can be found in the List of Features.
